Social Competition

We're excited that Bachata Sauna Festival will host the Finland Regional Bachata Social Competition organized by the widely known and highly respected Gregory & Karolina from Warsaw Bachata Meet Up.

They bring together the best dancers from all over the world, so whether you're a seasoned professional or an enthusiastic beginner, this is your chance to showcase your skills and experience the vibrant community.

Participation fee: 20€, paid to Warsaw Bachata Meetup at the festival

Please note that a full pass (for locals) or a party pass (for international visitors) is mandatory to participate.

World Bachata Social Competition

The semi-finalists and finalists from Bachata Sauna and other events in Poland, France, Germany, Hungary, Lithuania, Switzerland, Sweden, Denmark & more will participate in the WORLD FINALS on 29.11.2025 in Warsaw.


How it works?

Stage 1 - Initial Round

Round 1: All dancers perform simultaneously with different partners, judged individually by roaming judges.

Round 2: A judges' table is introduced, with the audience seated on the sides and back. Dancers perform in pairs alphabetically, changing partners frequently while dancing both an open and a competition music.

Stage 2 - Scoring

Dancers receive points from the judges and then they score performance of their peers.

Participants can vote for 3 of the best partners, which later receive additional score.

Stage 3 - Finals

Dancers perform with the judges, showcasing their skills in front of an expert panel.

It provides an opportunity for dancers to demonstrate their ability to adapt and excel, dancing alongside experienced judges who evaluate their technique, creativity, and interaction.

Stage 4 - World Finals

Finalists and semi-finalists from each regional competition are eligible to participate in the World Bachata Social Competition in Warsaw on 29.11.2025. The finalists from all over Europe compete for the championship title.
