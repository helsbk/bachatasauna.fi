Heymo 1

Budget hotel

Budget-friendly Metro to venue New building Modern design

Heymo 1 by Sokos Hotels offers budget-friendly and hassle-free accommodations. The hotel is conveniently located near the Keilaniemi metro station and scenic coastal views.

Address: Miestentie 5, 02150 Espoo

Transportation

  • Metro to venue: 30min including walking (single ticket 3.20€, multi-day tickets available)
  • Airport access: 55min total with train + metro (single ticket 4.40€)

Note: Earliest metro to the hotel from the venue departs 05:15 in the morning & last metro from the hotel to the venue departs at 23:30 in the evening. If you dance all night, you can easily use the metro. If you need to go back to the hotel during the night when the metro is not operating, we recommend Bolt/Uber (25€ to 30€ because alterantive public transportation options will take over an hour and require changing vehicles.
