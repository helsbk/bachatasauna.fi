Mastery Track

The exclusive 6 hour mastery track is meant for high level dancers who already have good foundations of bachata technique. Mandatory level screening and a maximum limit of 20 couples to ensure that the teachers can focus on advanced concepts:

  • 2 hours with Kornel & Catharine (Friday) focusing on musicality
  • 2 hours with Irene & Tomas (Saturday) focusing on linking sensual bachata techniques
  • 2 hours with Kornel & Catharine (Sunday) focusing on more musicality

IMPORTANT: You must be approved before registering for the mastery track. The workshops will not be useful if you don't already have a good basic step and some level of musicality.
