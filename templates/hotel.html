Hotel & Transportation

Noli, Sörnäinen (main hotel)

Noli Sörnäinen is a stylish aparthotel in Helsinki's Sörnäinen district, offering studios with amenities like a gym, sauna, co-working spaces, and communal areas, located in a vibrant area known for its urban arts and nightlife.

  • Walking distance to venue (700 meters)
  • 24h grocery store and many restaurants nearby
  • Airport bus stop next to hotel (35min total, 4.40€)
  • Easy public transportation access (bus, metro, tram) to explore Helsinki

Double bed rooms available from 175€ for 2 nights

Heymo 1, Otaniemi (budget hotel)

Heymo 1 by Sokos Hotels offers budget-friendly and hassle-free accommodations. It's a bit further away from the venue, but you can use the metro (or taxi). In return, the prices are significantly lower than at the main hotel.
