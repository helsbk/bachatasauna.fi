Hotel & Transportation

Main hotel: Noli

Noli Sörnäinen is a stylish aparthotel offering studios with amenities like a gym, sauna, and co-working spaces. It's located very close to the venue in a vibrant area known for its urban arts and nightlife. 700 meter walk to venue, 24 hour grocery store and many restaurants nearby, airport bus stop in front (35min total, 4.40€), and easy public transportation access (bus, metro, tram) to explore Helsinki.

Budget hotel: Heymo 1

Heymo 1 by Sokos Hotels offers budget-friendly and hassle-free accommodations. It's a bit further away from the venue, but you can use the metro (or taxi). In return, the prices are significantly lower than at the main hotel.
