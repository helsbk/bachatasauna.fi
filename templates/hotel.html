Hotels

Main hotel: Noli

Noli is a stylish aparthotel offering studios with amenities including a gym, sauna, and co-working spaces. It's only a 700 meter walk away the main venue in a vibrant area known for its urban arts and nightlife.

Prices from 90€/night for 2 persons.

Budget hotel: Heymo 1

Heymo 1 offers budget-friendly accommodations in a new, modern building. It's a bit further away from the venue, but you can easily use the metro (or taxi). In return, the prices are much lower than at the main hotel.

Prices from 60€/night for 2 persons, 80€/night for 3 persons, and 150€/night for 7 persons.
