Hotel & Transportation

Noli, Sörnäinen (main hotel)

Noli Sörnäinen is a stylish aparthotel in Helsinki's Sörnäinen district, offering studios with amenities like a gym, sauna, co-working spaces, and communal areas, located in a vibrant area known for its urban arts and nightlife.

  • Walking distance to venue (700 meters)
  • 24h grocery store and many restaurants nearby
  • Airport bus stop next to hotel (35min total, 4.40€)
  • Easy public transportation access (bus, metro, tram) to explore Helsinki

Double bed rooms available from 175€ for 2 nights

Heymo 1, Otaniemi (2nd hotel)

Heymo 1 by Sokos Hotels offers budget-friendly and hassle-free accommodations. The hotel is conveniently located near the Keilaniemi metro station and scenic coastal trails.

  • Metro to venue (30min including walking)
  • Airport access via train + metro (55min total, 4.40€)
  • Cheapest option

Note: Earliest metro to the hotel from the venue departs 05:15 in the morning & last metro from the hotel to the venue departs at 23:30 in the evening. If you dance all night, you can easily use the metro (single ticket 3.20€, multi-day tickets available). If you need to go back to the hotel during the night when the metro is not operating, we recommend Bolt/Uber for about 25€ - 30€ because alterantive public transportation options will take over an hour.

Twin bed rooms available from 140€ for 2 nights, triple bed rooms available from 180€ for 2 nights, 7 bed rooms available from 305€ for 2 nights.
