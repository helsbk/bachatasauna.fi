Humanitarian Aid

We would like to welcome you to donate 100% of the festival ticket price to help people who are actually in need. You can pay for your tickets as a donation to one of the following humanitarian aid organizations:

  • UNRWA
  • Red Cross
  • Doctors Without Borders
How?

When buying your ticket, select Donate to Humanitarian Aid as the payment option.

After that, you will have to manually make a one-time donation on one of the websites linked above. Please make sure to enter your full name. As the amount, enter the final ticket amount including all fees. As the email address, enter aid@bachatasauna.fi

Done!

IMPORTANT: Please be sure to use the email address aid@bachatasauna.fi on the donation page. Otherwise we will not be able to validate your donation.
