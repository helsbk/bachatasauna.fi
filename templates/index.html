Meet the Venue

500m2 venue ∙ 300m2 wooden dance floor ∙ Pro sound system ∙ Atmospheric party lighting ∙ Industrial ventilation fans ∙ Free water ∙ Free snacks ∙ Restaurant & Bar

Our workshops and parties on Friday - Sunday will be at Cirko, an excellent venue in an industrial area known for hosting some of the biggest festivals in Helsinki. We are social dancers first and foremost so we are making sure that Cirko will be the perfect place to dance all night long with all of the essentials well taken care of.

Venue address: Kaasutehtaankatu 1, 00540 Helsinki

Cirko can be easily reached with public transportation (metro, bus, and tram). Use the HSL journey planner to find the fastest route. We also offer free parking.

Cirko is conveniently located right next to the new Redi shopping center with lots of grocery stores and restaurants.
