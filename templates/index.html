Meet the Venue

Cirko is an excellent venue in an industrial area known for hosting some of the biggest festivals in Helsinki. We are social dancers first and foremost so we are making sure that it will be the perfect place to dance all night long with all of the essentials well taken care of. For example, we will be brining in huge industrial fans to make sure space is ventilated properly and we will be providing free water & snacks to help you stay energized and hydrated.

500m2 venue 300m2 wood floor Pro sound system Party lighting Industrial ventilation Restaurant Bar Free water Free snacks

Venue address: Kaasutehtaankatu 1, 00540 Helsinki

Cirko is conveniently located near lots of metro, bus, and tram connections. Use the HSL journey planner to find the fastest public transportation options or use our free parking if you're driving. The Redi shopping center is also right next to Cirko for quick access to lots of grocery stores and restaurants.
