Meet the Venue

The main venue on Friday, Saturday, and Sunday will be Cirko, an excellent venue in an industrial area known for hosting some of the biggest festivals in Helsinki. Cirko is perfect for our workshops & parties:

  • 500m2 venue with 300m2 wooden dance floor
  • Excellent party lighting & high quality sound system
  • Big industrial fans & air conditioners for cooling
  • Free water & snacks
  • Restaurant & bar in the same building
  • Restaurants & grocery stores 5min walk away at Redi shopping center
  • Lots of metro, tram, and bus options as well as free parking

Main venue address: Kaasutehtaankatu 1, 00540 Helsinki, Finland

Secondary venue is right next to the main venue.
